    {{ _('About') }} Find Data

    The '{{ g.site_title }}' catalogue exists to provide access to metadata, describing the data held across the Defra group, to make it findable.

    The metadata extracted from other systems is translated into a common format, and then made searchable. As a result of this aggregation it is possible to measure the quality of the metadata, the underlying data, the processes that led to the creation of this data as well as obtain a better understanding of the data Defra holds.
